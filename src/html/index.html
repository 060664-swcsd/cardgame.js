Uno
Uno is a card game where the goal is to get rid of all your cards.
To play: Get a group of people (or bots). You will get a couple of cards which will be either:

- color with number
- skip / plus 2 with color
- wildcard / wildcard +4
When you get down to 1 card, you better call out "Uno!" (by pressing the button). If you dont, you get 2 more cards.
